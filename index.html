6 Things to do when you visit in Brighton.

Ready to take a trip to Brighton? Find out the best places to visit, and top activities to participate in when you arrive.

Go on the Beach.

When the weather is hot (which is rare), you can go to the beach and swim or just sunbathe, and find various activities like Basketball or Volleyball.

Explore the Pier.

The Pier is one of the biggest tourist attractions: filled with many activities such as casinos, British food like Fish & Chips, and games.

Splash out at the Lagoon.

If you're a fan of water sports, then taking an adventurous trip to the lagoon can give you a refereshing splash of exciting.

Take a flight to the I360.

Take a flight on the I360, a big cylinder, with a glass doughnut around it, allowing you to get a whole view of the entirety of Brighton city.

Study at a Library.

If you're a student, and need to get some work done, you can take a visit to the Jubilee library close to the city centre, to get your studies done.

Eat at a Restaurant.

There are variety of different restaurants to choose from in Brighton. From Indian, Chinese, and traditional British foods.
